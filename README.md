# Resilience Building Architect
Designs personalized resilience-building practices for adversity navigation and psychological recovery.
## What it does
- Diagnoses your current resilience baseline across four domains: internal (beliefs/coping), social (support network), practical (material resources), and existential (meaning/purpose)
- Generates a tailored Resilience Practice Plan with daily and weekly micro-practices targeting your lowest-scoring domains
- Provides a 3-phase acute adversity recovery protocol: Stabilize (grounding), Process (meaning-making), Integrate (growth)
## Example scenarios
**1. 创业失败后怎么走出来**
> 👤 去年和朋友合伙开餐饮店，投了30万全亏了，现在钱没了朋友也没了，每天醒来就觉得人生没有意义。
> 🤖 创业失败后的心理重建方案：1）物理重启——先做一件简单且有成就感的事重建信心；2）认知重建——把'我失败了就是废物'转变为'我的这次创业尝试失败了，但我学到了宝贵经验'；3）行动方案——先找固定收入工作恢复经济，同时为下一次创业积累资源；4）支持系统——加入创业者互助群，和同样经历的人交流让你发现你不是一个人。

**2. 人生低谷后的恢复计划**
> 👤 "I've been through a rough year (divorce, layoff). Build me a resilience recovery plan."
> 🤖 Phased recovery plan — Stabilize (sleep, nutrition, movement basics), Process (structured reflection on both losses with meaning-making exercises), Rebuild (identity reconstruction beyond job/relationship), Grow (post-traumatic growth exploration) — each phase with daily practices and progress indicators.

**3. 培养孩子的抗挫能力**
> 👤 "My teenager gives up at the first sign of difficulty. How do I build their resilience?"
> 🤖 Parent's resilience-building guide — age-appropriate challenge calibration (optimal frustration zone), failure-normalization conversations (share your own failures), problem-solving coaching (ask, don't solve), effort-praise vs. outcome-praise scripts, and resilience-story library (books and films organized by challenge type).
